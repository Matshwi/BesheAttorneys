About

Beshe Attorneys Incorporated is a newly established law firm founded by Mr. Oko Beshe, its sole practitioner and director. Mr. Beshe began his LLB at Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University (now Nelson Mandela University) and completed it at the University of South Africa while gaining work experience. He completed his articles at Hildebrand Attorneys in Cape Town, a full-service firm serving German and international clients, and has experience in Consumer Protection, Insurance Law, and Environmental Law.
